# Ethics, Governance, and Limitations

## Ethics

This platform is designed for educational purposes and uses only synthetic data.

No real personal, medical, or confidential information should be stored in the system.

The platform provides decision support and does not replace human judgment.

Teachers, counselors, and administrators remain responsible for all final decisions.

---

## Privacy

Access to information is controlled through role-based permissions.

Users can only access data relevant to their responsibilities.

All important actions should be logged for audit purposes.

---

## Governance

The system follows the principle of least privilege:

- Teachers manage educational records.
- Counselors manage interventions and follow-up.
- Administrators manage users and platform settings.

Unauthorized actions must be blocked and recorded.

---

## Limitations

- Risk scores are indicators only.
- Alerts may contain false positives or false negatives.
- The platform depends on data quality.
- Human review is required before interventions.
- Results should not be interpreted as medical or psychological diagnoses.
