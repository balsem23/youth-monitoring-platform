Care Pathway

1. Observation & CSV Ingestion

Admins upload weekly student records; teachers record daily attendance.

2. Risk Assessment & Alert Generation

Algorithms flag high-risk students and automatically spawn school/health alerts.

3. Workflow Case Initialization

Teachers or admins open a workflow case for flagged students to monitor state progression.

4. Counselor Interventions & Close-out

Counselors create care plans, host appointments, transition cases, and close resolved workflows.
